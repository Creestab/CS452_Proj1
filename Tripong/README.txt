Game Rules:

Hit the bouncing square with your paddle to keep it from hitting your side of the field. Earn 2 points for scoring and lose 1 for letting the square in.


Controls:

Player 1 (Red)
	Up:	W
	Down:	S
Player 2 (Blue)
	Up:	[
	Down:	]
Player 3 (Green)
	Up:	Up Arrow
	Down:	Down Arrow
Player 4 (Yellow)
	Up:	V
	Down:	B
